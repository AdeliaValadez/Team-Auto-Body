About Us

Our Story

Team Auto Body's Commitment to Excellence.

Welcome to Team Auto Body! Since our launch on February 20, 2020, we've been dedicated to offering you the best in auto body care, backed by over 40 years of combined experience. Our founders opened this shop with a deep commitment to craftsmanship and a genuine desire to serve our community.

Located conveniently near James Madison University (JMU) and Eastern Mennonite University (EMU), we’re proud to be a trusted partner for both students and the local community. We focus on delivering quality work with a personal touch, making sure every vehicle we service reflects our high standards.

At Team Auto Body, we believe in fairness, transparency, and a personal touch. Our fair pricing and customer-focused approach guarantee that you’ll drive away not just satisfied, but thrilled. Experience the difference with Team Auto Body – where your car is treated like family, and excellence is our standard!

Meet the Team

Founders

Humberto & Daniel, the dynamic duo behind Team Auto Body, bring a wealth of experience and a shared passion for excellence to our shop. With over 40 years of combined expertise in the auto body industry, they have dedicated their careers to perfecting the craft of auto repair and enhancing customer satisfaction. Beto and Daniel, our Lead Technician's, apply their years of hands-on experience to every repair, ensuring meticulous attention to detail and top-quality results. Their technical skills and commitment to excellence are the foundation of our shop’s reputation.

Technicians
